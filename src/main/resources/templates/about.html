About Me

Hey! Looks like you've stumbled upon my about page, I'm so sorry.

I was born and raised in Lewisburg, Pennsylvania. My history with programming starts sometime around the age of six or seven; which was when I played my first computer game. The game was called Dark Forces, it was set in the Star Wars universe and you played as a rebel fighting against imperial forces. I remember being simultaneously amazed and terrified by the blaster-wielding stormtroopers, because it was just like playing pretend "army" with my friends, except to me these were ACTUAL stormtroopers trying to blow me up.

I've held a fascination for computers ever since then. It wasn't just a fascination in computer games either, I found myself wondering just what they were capable of, what was going on inside the machine, and how to not break them every time I tried to find out the answers to my inquiries. Many years and many fried motherboards later, I became knowledgeable about the hardware side of computing.

Nowadays I'm focused on improving my skills in software development. Originally I was worried that I'd become disillusioned with the field from working at "desk jobs," but instead I've found that coming up with unique and effective solutions can be an extremely rewarding process in itself. I look forward to growing as a developer so that one day my applications can only be explained with the word "magic."
